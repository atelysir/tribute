Painful Exit

Of

Mrs Chinwe

eulogies

LIFE AND TIMES OF LATE MRS. CHINWENDU MARYLYN VINCENT-ONYENEHIDO PREAMBLES:

According to the scriptures there is a time for everything; a time to born and a time to die Eccl 3:1-2a. Late Mrs. Chinwendu Marylyn Vincent Onyenehido came, saw and conquered.

BIRTH / EARLY DAYS:
Late Mrs. Chinwendu Marylyn Vincent Onyenehido, was born in the city of Aba Abia State on 4th June 1975, into the family of Late Elder and Elder EZINNE Mrs. Jerome Jakame Opara, she was their 4th child as her parents lost two children they had during the Nigeria – Biafra war. Her early days was very exciting as it was in her nature to be very sociable. She attended Okigwe Road Primary School and Girls Secondary Commercial School all in Aba Abia State. She furthered her studies at the University of Uyo, this was a tough venture as caused by early demise of her father Late Elder Jerome J. Opara. However ,in pursuit for a better future she learned tailoring and later moved to Lagos where she met her lovely husband Mr. Vincent.

MARRIED LIFE:
Late Mrs. Chinwendu Marylyn Vincent Onyenehido, met her husband in Lagos in her search for a greener pasture. They married in Nov. 1998. She happily lived with her husband through the thick and thin of their lives journey. Their union was blessed with five lovely children.

SOCIAL LIFE / ACHIEVEMENT:
Late Mrs. Chinwe Vin as she was fondly called by her acquaintances, was the life of any party she attended. Being very jovial and easy to relate with, anyone that come into contact with her experienced her kindness and liberal heart. She identified with some social groups during her life time and made positive contribution to the groups she belonged. To mention a few, She was a member of Imo Welfare Association where she served as the Financial Secretary in the Women Wing. She also was a part of Ohaneze Ndigbo Badagry Office and served as the Organizing Secretary. At every point in her social life, she participated in her best capacity per time. The good work and the name she had will ever remain in the heart of the people that crossed her path in her journey to the other side of eternity.

CHRISTIAN LIFE
Late Mrs. Chinwendu Marylyn Vincent Onyenehido, was born into a catholic family and surrendered her life to Jesus Christ at Assemblies of God Church in Aba during the period when the light of God entered her father’s house through her younger brother. She later joined The Redeemed Christian Church of God in Lagos. She served as the Secretary RCCG Living Water Tower Lagos Province 31 and served God in various capacity until it pleased God to call her home.

HER DEATH:
Apostle Paul said in Phil. 1:21 “For me to live is Christ to die is gain.” As it is not in our place to question God regarding His Supreme will. If it were for love, care and money Late Mrs. Chinwendu Maryn Vincent Onyenehido, would still be here with us. It pleased the Lord to call her home after a brief battle with sickness, on Friday 17th February, 2023 at UNTH Enugu the Lord called her home unto His rest prepared for those that love Him.

CONCLUSION:
We are in pain that you are no longer here with us, we are comforted that you are in better place where there is no darkness, sickness and insecurity etc. Those left to mourn her; her husband, Children Brothers, Sisters, Nephews, Nieces, Uncles, Aunties and a host of friends and well-wishers.
Adieu, Chinwe
Adieu Mumy
Adieu, Daa Chi
Adieu, Big Mumy
Continue to rest in the bosom of the Lord until the resurrection morning, when we will meet again to part no more. ADIEU!!!
